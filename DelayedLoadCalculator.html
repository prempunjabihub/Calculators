How this calculation works:

  • Cycle 1 (Start): Person A drives a cheap car (value = initial capital). They invest the equivalent of Person B's EMI minus the monthly repair costs.
  • Cycle 2+ (Later): Person A buys the new car with accumulated cash. They continue to invest the amount Person B is paying in EMI. Since A now drives a new car too, their maintenance costs are assumed to be equal to B's.
  • The Result: The "Cash Balance" shown is what Person A has remaining in the bank immediately after buying the brand new car with cash.
